# Company Background Framework

The **Company Background** framework helps users document and analyze the history, mission, vision, and key details of a company. It also identifies strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities based on the company’s background. The system can gather publicly available data to assist in completing this framework.

## Purpose
The Company Background framework helps teams:
- Document the history, mission, vision, and key details of a company.
- Analyze the company’s strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities.
- Provide a foundation for understanding the company’s position in its industry.

## Sections
The framework includes the following components:

1. **Company Overview**: Provide a high-level summary of the company, including:
   - Name
   - Industry
   - Headquarters
   - Founding Year
2. **Mission and Vision**:
   - Mission Statement: The company’s purpose and core values.
   - Vision Statement: The company’s long-term goals and aspirations.
3. **History**: Summarize the company’s key milestones and achievements.
4. **Products and Services**: Describe the company’s main offerings.
5. **Market Position**:
   - Target Market: The primary audience the company serves.
   - Competitive Position: The company’s standing relative to competitors.
6. **Strengths, Weaknesses, and Opportunities**:
   - Strengths: Internal advantages or unique capabilities.
   - Weaknesses: Internal challenges or limitations.
   - Opportunities: External factors the company can leverage.

## Interaction Methods
Users can interact with the Company Background framework in three ways:
1. **Upload Files**: Upload company reports, presentations, or other documents to auto-fill sections of the framework.
2. **Chat with AI**: Answer questions from the AI agent or allow the system to gather publicly available data to complete the framework.
3. **Guided Steps**: Follow detailed instructions to manually complete each section.

## Output
The completed Company Background framework will be presented as:
- A structured document summarizing the company’s background, strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities.
- A downloadable PDF version of the framework.
- An option to save the framework to the user’s profile for future reference or reuse.
