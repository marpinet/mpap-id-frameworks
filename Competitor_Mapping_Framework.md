# Competitor Mapping Framework

The **Competitor Mapping** framework helps users analyze competitors’ offerings, market positions, and value propositions. Inspired by the Buyer Utility Map, it identifies opportunities for differentiation and highlights market threats using hard data.

## Purpose
The Competitor Mapping framework helps teams:
- Map competitors’ offerings against one another to identify strengths and weaknesses.
- Highlight opportunities for differentiation and innovation.
- Analyze market threats based on competitors’ positions and value creation.

## Sections
The framework includes the following components:

1. **Competitor Overview**:
   - Name of Competitor
   - Industry/Market Segment
   - Key Offerings
   - Market Position (e.g., market share, revenue, customer base)

2. **Buyer Utility Analysis**:
   - Map competitors’ offerings across the Buyer Utility Map dimensions:
     - **Utility Levers**: Performance, simplicity, convenience, risk reduction, fun/image, environmental friendliness.
     - **Buyer Experience Stages**: Purchase, delivery, use, supplements, maintenance, disposal.
   - Identify areas where competitors create value and where gaps exist.

3. **Opportunities for Differentiation**:
   - Highlight areas where the user’s offering can stand out.
   - Identify unmet needs or underserved buyer experience stages.

4. **Market Threats**:
   - Analyze competitors’ strengths that pose risks to the user’s offering.
   - Highlight areas where competitors dominate the Buyer Utility Map.

## Interaction Methods
Users can interact with the Competitor Mapping framework in three ways:
1. **Upload Files**: Upload competitor analysis reports or market data to auto-fill sections of the framework.
2. **Chat with AI**: Answer questions from the AI agent or allow the system to gather publicly available data to complete the framework.
3. **Guided Steps**: Follow detailed instructions to manually complete each section.

## Output
The completed Competitor Mapping framework will be presented as:
- A structured document summarizing competitor analysis, opportunities, and threats.
- A visual map inspired by the Buyer Utility Map.
- A downloadable PDF version of the framework.
- An option to save the framework to the user’s profile for future reference or reuse.
