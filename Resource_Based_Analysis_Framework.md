# Resource-Based Analysis with the 8 Capitals of Innovation Framework

The **Resource-Based Analysis with the 8 Capitals of Innovation** framework helps users assess and categorize the resources available to their organization across eight key capitals. It also identifies leverage points, opportunities, and gaps for maximizing the impact of these resources.

## Purpose
The Resource-Based Analysis framework helps teams:
- Assess and categorize resources across the 8 capitals of innovation.
- Identify leverage points, opportunities, and gaps for each capital.
- Develop a comprehensive understanding of resource availability and potential.

## Sections
The framework includes the following sections, one for each capital:

1. **Financial Capital**: Assess monetary resources, funding, and financial stability.
   - Leverage Points: Key areas where financial resources can be maximized.
   - Opportunities: Potential ways to increase or better utilize financial resources.
   - Gaps: Areas where financial resources are lacking or insufficient.

2. **Political Capital**: Evaluate influence, relationships, and alignment with policies or regulations.
   - Leverage Points, Opportunities, and Gaps.

3. **Social Capital**: Analyze networks, relationships, and community engagement.
   - Leverage Points, Opportunities, and Gaps.

4. **Digital Capital**: Assess technological assets, digital infrastructure, and data capabilities.
   - Leverage Points, Opportunities, and Gaps.

5. **Natural Capital**: Evaluate environmental resources, sustainability practices, and ecological impact.
   - Leverage Points, Opportunities, and Gaps.

6. **Manufactured Capital**: Assess physical assets, infrastructure, and tools.
   - Leverage Points, Opportunities, and Gaps.

7. **Cultural Capital**: Analyze cultural assets, values, and identity.
   - Leverage Points, Opportunities, and Gaps.

8. **Human Capital**: Evaluate skills, knowledge, and expertise within the organization.
   - Leverage Points, Opportunities, and Gaps.

## Interaction Methods
Users can interact with the Resource-Based Analysis framework in three ways:
1. **Upload Files**: Upload documents or resource inventories to auto-fill sections of the framework.
2. **Chat with AI**: Answer questions from the AI agent to generate a completed framework.
3. **Guided Steps**: Follow detailed instructions to manually complete each section.
