# Strategic Purpose Framework

The **Strategic Purpose** framework helps users define a clear and concise purpose statement that aligns with their organization’s goals and values. It also articulates the contribution, impact, and specific actions (jobs to be done) that the organization will undertake to achieve its purpose.

## Purpose
The Strategic Purpose framework helps teams:
- Define a clear and concise purpose statement.
- Align the organization’s goals and values with its strategic direction.
- Articulate the contribution, impact, and jobs to be done to achieve the organization’s purpose.

## Sections
The framework includes the following components:

1. **Purpose Statement**:
   - Define the organization’s purpose using the following structure:
     - "Our purpose is to [contribution] so that [impact] by [jobs to be done]."
   - Example: "Our purpose is to empower small businesses with digital tools so that they can thrive in a competitive marketplace by providing accessible and affordable technology solutions."

2. **Contribution**:
   - Describe the specific contribution the organization makes to its stakeholders or the world.
   - Examples: Providing innovative solutions, creating sustainable practices, fostering community growth.

3. **Impact**:
   - Articulate the broader impact or change the organization aims to achieve.
   - Examples: Improving quality of life, reducing environmental harm, driving economic growth.

4. **Jobs to Be Done**:
   - Define the specific actions or tasks the organization will undertake to fulfill its purpose.
   - Examples: Developing user-friendly software, offering training programs, building partnerships.

5. **Alignment with Goals and Values**:
   - Ensure the purpose statement aligns with the organization’s long-term goals and core values.
   - Provide a checklist or criteria for alignment.

## Interaction Methods
Users can interact with the Strategic Purpose framework in three ways:
1. **Upload Files**: Upload mission statements, strategic plans, or organizational values to auto-fill sections of the framework.
2. **Chat with AI**: Answer questions from the AI agent to generate a purpose statement and supporting details.
3. **Guided Steps**: Follow detailed instructions to manually complete each section.

## Output
The completed Strategic Purpose framework will be presented as:
- A structured document summarizing the purpose statement, contribution, impact, and jobs to be done.
- A downloadable PDF version of the framework.
- An option to save the framework to the user’s profile for future reference or reuse.
